Cat Trinket Plate Tutorial:

Materials Needed:

  • Airdry clay
  • Rolling pin
  • Craft knife
  • Water
  • Paint brushes
  • Optional: Modge Podge (or any type of gloss finisher)
    • White paint
    • Black paint
    • Orange paint
    • Brown paint
    • Pink paint

Instructions:

  1. On a flat surface, roll out your clay until even and about 2 cm thick.
  2. Cut around a 5x5 in square.
  3. Take the square and carve it into a cat face shape.
  4. Once you have the desired cat shape, take more clay and roll it out sideways so you have a long round piece of clay, about 1 inch thick.
  5. Take your rolled clay and place it on the borders of your cat clay design.
  6. Now with your hands and a little water, take your thumb and press the edge of the rolled clay into the bottom clay design to "mold" the rolled clay with the bottom clay smoothly.
  7. Go around and do that throughout the whole cat face.
  8. Let it sit and wait 24 hours or until it is completely dry
  9. Take some white paint and mix a little brown paint to get a beige color. Paint the whole cat the beige color.
  10. Take the orange paint and paint the bottom third orange, like the picture.
  11. Take the black and with the white, create grey paint and paint the top third black, like the picture.
  12. Take the pink paint and paint little curved triangles in the ear sections of the cat. Add a little heart in the center, lower bottom of the plate to resemble the cat's mouth.
  13. Take the black paint and create two circles evenly apart in the middle of the tray to portray the cat's eyes. You may sketch it out first.
  14. Let it sit until the paint completely dries.
  15. Optional: For a glossy finished look, take your Mod Podge glosser and paint a thin layer all around the cat and let it sit until it dries.
