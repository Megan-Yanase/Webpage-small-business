Flower Tutorial:

Materials Needed:

  • Air Dry Clay (any color)
  • White Air Dry Clay
  • Tooth Pick

Instructions:

  1. Warm both clays up in your hands by kneading them until they are moldable.
  2. Separate the colored clay that you want your flower to be into 5 equal parts.
  3. Roll those 5 parts into equal balls.
  4. Align the 5 balls in a flower design and carefully apply pressure to stick them together.
  5. Once they are together in a flower shape, take the toothpick and create 2 slightly diagonal lines a centimeter apart from each other on each petal.
  6. Now take the white clay and mold it into a circle slightly bigger than the petals.
  7. Place the white ball in the middle, applying a bit of pressure to make it stick.
  8. Let it sit and wait 24 hours or until it is completely dry.
