Welcome to Handmade Harmony!

Crafting Calmly, Creating Beautifully

Handmade Harmony was created to offer a creative outlet through air dry clay, allowing individuals to express their artistic side while crafting beautiful pieces. This therapeutic activity not only helps to destress but also provides a peaceful escape from the demands of daily life. By engaging in mindful crafting, you can give yourself a much-needed break and find harmony in the process.

Flowers

Difficulty: Easy

Create charming and simple air dry clay flowers that are perfect for beginners! These delightful flowers are easy to make and add a touch of whimsy to any project. Follow this step-by-step guide to craft your own adorable clay blossoms.

Cat Trinket Plate

Difficulty: Medium

Create a charming cat trinket plate using air dry clay, perfect for holding small treasures. Ideal for medium skill levels, this project features a round base with raised edges and a cute cat face in the center, complete with painted details.

Totoro Mini Calendar

Difficulty: Hard

Create an adorable Totoro mini calendar with air dry clay. This challenging project involves sculpting detailed Totoro figures and crafting miniature calendar pieces. Perfect for Studio Ghibli fans, it will enhance your workspace or home decor.
